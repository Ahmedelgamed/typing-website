Understanding Your Typing Speed Results

WPM (Words Per Minute) is the standard measurement for typing speed. Here's how different WPM scores compare:

  • 10-20 WPM: Beginner level
  • 20-40 WPM: Below average
  • 40-60 WPM: Average typing speed
  • 60-80 WPM: Above average
  • 80+ WPM: Excellent typing speed
